📘 Beginner Teaching Guide

  • Green Curve: Indicates rising momentum – potential buy signal.
  • Red Curve: Indicates declining momentum – potential sell or avoid.
  • Above 0.8: Overbought zone – watch for reversal or profit-taking.
  • Below -0.8: Oversold zone – look for bounce or trend change.
  • Best entry zone: When green curve is rising from below -0.8 toward 0.0, especially if multiple timeframes align.

📈 Suggested Entry Plan

  • 🎯 Entry: Watch 1M and 5M for bounce confirmation (wait for green curve up)
  • 🛑 Stop Loss: Below most recent low on 15M chart
  • 🏁 Target: Exit before 4H oscillator enters overbought (> 0.8)
  • ⚠️ Avoid entries when 1M/5M are falling, even if 4H looks bullish

🧠 Pro Tip: Time = Signal Maturity

  • Shorter timeframes (1M/5M): Good for early entries, quick exits
  • Longer timeframes (4H/1D): Higher accuracy, but take time to confirm
  • Stacking signals across timeframes = more reliable trade setup

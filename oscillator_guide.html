🧠 Momentum Oscillator Reading Guide

🎨 Color Codes

  • Green: Momentum is rising — possible long signal
  • Red: Momentum is falling — watch for reversal or take profits
  • Flat or Gray: Neutral / transition phase

📊 Value Zones

Value RangeZone MeaningSuggested Action
> +0.8OverboughtTake Profit or Short Setup
0.0 to +0.8Bullish MomentumStay in Long or Wait for Pullback
-0.8 to 0.0Neutral to BearishAvoid or Watch Closely
< -0.8OversoldPotential Long Setup Soon

✅ Easy Strategy for Beginners

  1. Go Long: when curve turns green below -0.8 and volume supports it.
  2. Take Profit: when curve turns red above +0.8.
  3. Stay Out: if curve is flat near 0 — this is a chop zone.
  4. Confirm: with multiple timeframes (1m, 5m, 15m).

⏱️ Timing Estimate

Each full wave (bottom to top to bottom) usually takes:

  • On 1-minute chart: ~3–5 minutes
  • On 5-minute chart: ~10–15 minutes
  • On 15-minute chart: ~30–45 minutes
